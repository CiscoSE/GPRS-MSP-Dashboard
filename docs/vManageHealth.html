vManageHealth module¶

vManageHealth.get_data()¶

Returns a list of dictionaries containing the health of the network and the devices in the network. The first dictionary contains the health of the network and the second dictionary contains the health of the devices. The health of the network is determined by the health of the devices in the network. The health of the devices is determined by the reachability of the devices.

param: None

Returns: [dict, list]

[networkHealth, deviceHealth]
